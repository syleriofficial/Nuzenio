Corrections

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Nuzenio is a live news discovery platform. We link to original publishers and clearly separate source links from AI summaries and context. If something is wrong, we want it fixed quickly.

What We Correct

  • Incorrect source attribution or publisher names.
  • Broken original story links or wrong article destinations.
  • AI summary, key fact, or context errors.
  • Wrong category, location, video, or live stream labeling.
  • Commercial or affiliate placement labeling issues.

How To Request A Correction

Email correction details to syleriofficial@gmail.com with the Nuzenio page URL, original publisher URL, and a short explanation of the issue.

Nuzenio does not rewrite original publisher reporting. Corrections on Nuzenio apply to our attribution, summaries, labels, links, and presentation.

Review Process

We review correction requests for clear evidence, publisher source context, and user impact. High-impact errors should be prioritized before lower-impact formatting or labeling issues.
