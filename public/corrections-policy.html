Corrections Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Nuzenio corrects its own presentation layer: source attribution, links, labels, summaries, AI context, editorial notes, and original content. We do not silently rewrite the work of original publishers.

Correction Priorities

  • High-impact factual, attribution, or safety errors.
  • Broken original publisher links or incorrect publisher names.
  • AI summary errors or missing context labels.
  • Incorrect sponsored, affiliate, opinion, or fact-check labeling.
  • Original Nuzenio article updates, revisions, and approval history.

How To Submit

Email syleriofficial@gmail.com with the Nuzenio URL, original publisher URL if available, evidence, and a short explanation.

Corrections should be logged in the admin dashboard so Nuzenio can keep review status, updated timestamps, and editorial accountability visible.
