About Nuzenio

Nuzenio is a multilingual news platform built for readers who want fast access to local, national, world, live, and video news in one clean experience.

The platform organizes public news feeds, playable video sources, and publisher attribution so readers can understand what happened quickly and continue to the original source for full reporting.

Nuzenio is English-first, with news-language controls for readers who prefer Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, Urdu, Arabic, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, or Korean.
