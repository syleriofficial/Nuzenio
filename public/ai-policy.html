AI Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Nuzenio uses AI-assisted reading features to help users understand live news faster. AI features are designed as summaries and context, not as a replacement for original publisher reporting.

How AI Is Used

  • To summarize article headlines and RSS descriptions.
  • To explain what happened and why a story may matter.
  • To organize visible key facts from available article metadata.
  • To help users compare source-attributed stories across categories.

Source Attribution

Every article view keeps the publisher name and original source link visible. Users should rely on the original publisher page for the complete report, quotes, images, legal details, and updates.

AI summaries on Nuzenio are reading aids. They may be incomplete or need correction when source feeds change, publisher metadata is limited, or a story develops quickly.

Corrections

If an AI summary, key fact, category, or context panel appears inaccurate, send the Nuzenio page URL and original publisher URL to syleriofficial@gmail.com.
