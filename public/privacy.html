Last updated: June 18, 2026

Nuzenio uses live RSS feeds, Google Analytics, Google AdSense, and may use Supabase authentication, saved article features, reading history, newsletter forms, or affiliate placements.

When you log in or subscribe, we may store your email, profile details provided by your authentication provider, saved articles, reading history, and newsletter subscription details.

Google AdSense and affiliate partners may use their own tracking technologies according to their policies. Paid links must be labeled, and editorial content remains separate from monetized placements.

Contact the site owner for deletion or data access requests.
