Privacy Policy

Nuzenio uses live RSS feeds, Supabase authentication, saved article features, reading history, newsletter forms, analytics events, and advertising or affiliate placements.

Advertising and affiliate partners may use their own tracking technologies after they are configured. Paid links must be labeled and editorial content remains separate from monetized placements.

Contact the site owner for deletion or data access requests.
