Editorial Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Nuzenio presents news discovered from public feeds and approved playable sources. Each story keeps source attribution and a link to the original publisher.

AI summaries, key facts, and context panels are reading aids. They are separated from the original source link and should be checked against the publisher article for important decisions.

Commercial placements, affiliate links, and sponsorships must be labeled and kept separate from editorial RSS coverage. Corrections should be made promptly when source attribution, summaries, or links are inaccurate.
